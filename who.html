Who We Are

SmileCare Network is a passionate team of dental professionals, volunteers, and community advocates dedicated to making dental care accessible to everyone. Our story began with a simple belief: that a healthy smile should be a right, not a privilege.

Our Vision

A world where every person has access to quality dental care, regardless of their economic status or geographic location.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, compassionate dental care to underserved communities through innovative programs and dedicated volunteers.

Our Values

Compassion, integrity, excellence, and community. We believe in treating every patient with dignity and respect.

Our Story

Founded in 2010 by Dr. Sarah Johnson, SmileCare Network began as a small mobile dental clinic serving rural communities in the Midwest. Dr. Johnson, a practicing dentist for over 15 years, was moved by the stories of patients who couldn't afford basic dental care.

What started as a weekend volunteer effort has grown into a comprehensive network of dental professionals, community partners, and dedicated volunteers serving thousands of patients across multiple states.

Today, our organization continues to expand its reach, bringing hope and healthy smiles to communities that need it most. Every patient we serve reminds us why we do what we do.
